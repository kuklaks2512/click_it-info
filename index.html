How to play

To go to the game page, click the "PLAY" button

Then, press start on the top right corner of the screen.

After the game ends, you can see your results in the scoreboard.

How it works

You have Game time,click counter and MRT(middle reaction time)

You have 1.5 seconds to click the square

To stop the game,just wait 1.5s and click the last square
